Use this page as the public App Store support URL after it is hosted on a stable HTTPS domain.

Contact

For account, deletion, privacy, purchase, or technical support, contact the app owner through the email or contact method listed on the App Store product page.

When requesting help, include:

  • Your Beep ID, if available.
  • The device model and iOS version.
  • The approximate time the issue happened.
  • Whether the issue involves login, Beep, Blink, widget, purchase, or account deletion.

Do not send passwords, Apple private keys, Supabase tokens, App Store Connect keys, service-role keys, or payment card details.

Account Deletion

If you can access the app, open My, then Account settings, then Delete Account. If you cannot access the app, use the public account deletion request page configured for the production release.
